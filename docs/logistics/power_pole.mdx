Energy transmission poles can be used to transmit power across large distances. They're usually built on elevated platforms, and can only be linked to other transmission poles.
To link them, use a target designator item (shift-click on the first one, then do the same on the second one to link them). A single pole can link to any number of poles. Any interconnected
poles will also share energy, allowing you to build large networks.

<center>
<ModAsset location="oritech:area/power_pole" width={512} />
</center>

Energy can be inserted and extracted from any pole within an interconnected transmission pole network. Energy can be inserted at the green port, and extracted at the red port. The maximum
transmission rate across the network is limited to 1M RF/t.

<Callout variant="info">
    The Transmission Poles are designed for long-range energy transport only, but also don't have infinite range. They need to be build at least 50 blocks apart to connected, and
    have a max range of 1000 blocks.
</Callout>

A pipe wrench can be used to zipline along power pole cables. To do so, simply click on a cable with a wrench in hand.

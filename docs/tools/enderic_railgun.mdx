The enderic railgun is a powerful ranged weapon that fires a projectile at high speed.
It is capable of dealing significant damage to enemies and can be used to break blocks at a distance.
The railgun is powered by RF, and must be charged to function.

Holding left mouse (e.g. attack/mine) fires a continuous beam that damages any entities in its path, and also mines the first block in line.
Energy will be transferred to an energy containing block instead of breaking it. It can also transform mined blocks, similar to the enderic laser (e.g. fluxite mining).

Mining-Mode / block destruction can be toggled of by shift+right-clicking with the item in hand.

Using the right mouse button fires a powerful projectile that explodes at the target location, destroying nearby blocks and entities.
